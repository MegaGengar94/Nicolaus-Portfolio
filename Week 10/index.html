Pizza

Prep. time: 15 min | Cook time: 20 min | Serves: 8

INGREDIENTS:

  • 1 pizza dough prepared and pre-baked for 5 min
  • ½ cup pesto sauce
  • baby spinach or arugula
  • zucchini, sliced
  • purple onion sliced
  • bell pepper sliced
  • cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup shredded
  • ¼ cup freshly grated parmesan
  • Other topping ideas: artichoke, mushrooms, asparagus, broccoli
